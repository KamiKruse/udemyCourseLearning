Vivek's Best Video Games of all Time

My top 3 games of in no particular order

Metal Gear Solid

Its a Hideo Kojima game. Fantastic story, 4th wall breaking game mechanics, music, sound and impressive technological feat

Nier Automata

Haunting world and music. Great story. And even more amazing and varied gameplay

God of War

Story of a warrior wronged by the gods and his fall, ascenion, and later redemption, makes it one of the best video games of all time
